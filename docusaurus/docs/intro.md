# Welcome to Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics

Welcome to this interactive textbook on **Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics**. This resource is designed to provide you with a comprehensive understanding of how artificial intelligence is being embodied in physical systems, particularly humanoid robots.

## What You Will Learn

This textbook covers the essential concepts, technologies, and applications that are shaping the future of robotics:

- **Physical AI Fundamentals** - Understanding AI systems that interact with the physical world
- **Humanoid Robotics** - The design and engineering of human-like robots
- **Sensors and Perception** - How robots sense and understand their environment
- **Actuators and Movement** - The mechanisms that enable robot motion
- **AI/ML Integration** - Connecting machine learning with physical systems
- **Real-World Applications** - Case studies and practical implementations

## How to Use This Textbook

This textbook is designed to be read sequentially, with each chapter building on concepts from previous chapters. However, you can also jump to specific topics using the sidebar navigation.

**Estimated Total Reading Time:** 45 minutes (approximately 7-8 minutes per chapter)

### Your Progress

As you read through the chapters, your progress will be tracked automatically. You can see how far you've come and pick up where you left off on your next visit.

### Offline Reading

Once you've visited a chapter, it will be available for offline reading. This is perfect for studying during commutes or in areas with limited connectivity.

## Who Is This For?

This textbook is designed for:

- **Students** exploring robotics and AI as a field of study
- **Developers** interested in building AI-powered physical systems
- **Enthusiasts** curious about the future of humanoid robotics
- **Professionals** looking to understand the intersection of AI and robotics
